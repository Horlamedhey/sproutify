What is Sproutify?

Sproutify alludes to a specific cryptocurrency farming method that relies on Uniswap trading fees to create a non-inflationary and highly-profitable farming system for traders. Sproutify uses a new innovative cryptocurrency farming called Trans-farming.
Trans-farming is a unique feature that enables users to recoup their Uniswap trading fees by automatically refunding the equivalent amount in SPRT tokens on SPRT Uniswap Buy Trades. The token Contract is already deployed to Ethereum mainnet.

The first Comprehensive Non-inflationary Trans-farming Protocol.

Trans-farming Incentive

The key benefit of Trans-farming is that it gives users the ability to offset their transaction fees by earning SPRT token (a trade farming token).
"Sproutify creates profits for farmers from its ongoing series of transactions and infuse it into the market to provide a constant asymptotic upward pressure."

Sproutify Goal

The future goals for the Sproutify platform can be summarized as SPRT Liquidity Amplification and are listed below:
1) SPRT Price Increase.
2) Ecosystem Sustainability.
Liquidity Amplifier dapps are used for SPRT token buybacks and adding to the SPRT liquidity pool. Liquidity Amplifier Dapps could be of several forms, such as SPRT LP token staking for NFT tokens or gaming dapps that generates funds to achieve the purpose of SPRT Uniswap Liquidity increase.
